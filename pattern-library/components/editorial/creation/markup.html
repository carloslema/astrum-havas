When pitching or creating remember to view content for the site and our readers through a lens of applicable value. Buck geocentric stereotypes and provide stories about topics that are interesting, relatable, and informative.

Download specific style guidelines:

  • Try to embed at least one deep link in each article with anchor text that links to a specific article on the Download. The anchor text should be short (i.e. four words) and descriptive.
  • For SEO optimization, use ampersands in all headlines, rather than the word and.
  • All links must open a new window.
  • Do not discuss client work that has not been released without written permission of the client.
  • Use American English spelling, not British.
